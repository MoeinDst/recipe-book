# Mixed Nuts

## Ingredients

- walnuts
- pecans
- almonds
- cranberries
- raisins
- cheese (feta/goat)
- balsamic or lemon vinaigrette


## Instructions

- Step 1: Toast nuts for 2-3 mins in a dry pan.
- Step 2:  Toast nuts for 2-3 mins in a dry pan (optional). Combine all ingredients in a bowl and toss.
